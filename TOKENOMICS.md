## Earning Mechanism — "Proof of Build"

BVLT can only be earned by satisfying **all three conditions simultaneously**. This prevents farming and protects price stability.

### Three requirements (must meet all at once)

1. **Stake OPN** — minimum 10 OPN locked for 30 days
2. **Deploy a verified contract** — source code must be verified on OPN Chain explorer
3. **Wait lock period** — 7 days after deploy before claiming rewards

### Reward schedule

| Activity | BVLT Earned |
|---|---|
| Deploy 1 verified contract | +100 BVLT |
| Lock Commitment (deposit OPN) | +50 BVLT |
| First wallet connect (once) | +25 BVLT |
| 5 deploys milestone | +250 BVLT bonus |
| 10 deploys milestone | +500 BVLT bonus |
| 25 deploys milestone | +1,000 BVLT bonus |

## 3 Core Token Functions

### 1. Gated Access — spend BVLT to unlock premium features

| Feature | Cost |
|---|---|
| Builder Profile NFT | 500 BVLT |
| Portfolio NFT | 750 BVLT |
| Verified Builder Badge | 300 BVLT |
| AI Mentor (monthly) | 1,000 BVLT/month |
| Create Guild | 2,500 BVLT |

### 2. Fee Sharing — hold BVLT to earn passive income

- 50% of all platform fees flow into the Revenue Pool
- Distributed proportionally to all BVLT stakers every 30 days
- More stake = larger share of revenue
- No minimum holding period to participate

### 3. Staking Yield — stake BVLT for dynamic APY

- Stake BVLT into the vault to earn yield from the builder OPN deposit pool
- APY is dynamic and self-regulating:
  - More stakers → APY decreases
  - Fewer stakers → APY increases
- This natural equilibrium protects token price over time

## Burn Mechanism — 50/50 Split

Every time BVLT is spent to unlock a feature, the tokens are split:

| Destination | Share | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Burned forever | 50% | Permanently removed from supply → deflationary pressure |
| Treasury | 50% | Recycled as future builder rewards → platform sustainability |

This mirrors the model used by leading DeFi protocols (Uniswap, Aave) — balancing deflation with long-term sustainability.
